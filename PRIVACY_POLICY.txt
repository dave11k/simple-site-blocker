PRIVACY POLICY FOR SIMPLE SITE BLOCKER

Last Updated: July 5, 2025

1. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT

Simple Site Blocker collects and stores the following data locally on your device:
- Website blocking profiles (lists of websites you choose to block)
- Distraction notes (text notes you write during blocking sessions)
- Math difficulty preference (easy, medium, or hard)
- Blocking session state (active/inactive status and timer information)

2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

All data collected by Simple Site Blocker is:
- Stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage
- Used solely to provide the extension's functionality
- Never transmitted to external servers or third parties
- Never shared, sold, or distributed to any other parties

3. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

- All data is stored locally on your device only
- No data is sent to remote servers or cloud services
- No personal identifying information is collected
- No tracking or analytics are performed

4. HOW TO ACCESS, MODIFY, OR DELETE YOUR DATA

You can manage your data at any time through the extension:
- View/edit blocking profiles in the Settings page
- View/clear distraction notes in the Settings page
- Reset all extension data using the "Clear All Data" button in Settings
- Uninstall the extension to remove all data permanently

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Simple Site Blocker does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

This extension does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

7. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in the extension's updated version.

8. CONTACT

For privacy-related questions, please visit the extension's GitHub repository or Chrome Web Store listing.

This privacy policy ensures your data remains private and secure, stored only on your device.
